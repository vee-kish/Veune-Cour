About Vanessa

The curious mind and steady hands behind Veune Studio.

Vanessa Kinya is a frontend developer and visual storyteller who loves weaving nature‑inspired motion into modern interfaces. Her work balances technical polish with soft, welcoming aesthetics.

At Veune Cour, Vanessa partners with eco‑friendly brands, creators, and founders to build digital spaces that feel like calm clearings in a noisy web — places where stories can breathe and users feel gently guided.

When she is not designing interfaces, you will find her sketching foliage in the margins of notebooks, tweaking color palettes, or experimenting with new micro‑interaction patterns.

  • Focus UX, UI, Frontend
  • Style Pastel, naturistic, playful
  • Based in Remote · Open to global
  • Available for 2026 collaborations
